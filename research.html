Algebraic Geometry of Curves

My PhD research is in the Arithmetic Geometry of Curves, predominantly the Lifting Problem.

The Lifting Problem asks which curves with finite group actions, defined over characteristic p, lift to characteristic 0? The idea behind this question is to relate geometry of curves over characteristic p to characteristic 0, thereby gaining new insights to familiar objects like curves over the complex numbers.

The Local Lifting Problem transfers the above question to a local case, involving groups acting on power series rings. This problem is easier to solve than the global case above, and allows for more techniques to be used. My current developments have been toward the local lifting problem for the group of Quaternions.
