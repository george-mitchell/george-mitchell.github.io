## Bio and Education

I am a Doctoral Lecturer in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at John Jay College of Criminal Justice.

I hold a PhD in Mathematics from the City University of New York, USA, in the field of _Arithmetric Geometry_, and a MMath from Newcastle University, UK.

This page also hosts my blog about mathematical statistics, causal inference and machine learning.
